AUTONOMOUS CAR

A car that can drive itself and all you would have to do is tell it where to go.

The self-driving car is one of the projects in progress at Astra robotics. The team is working on developing an autonomous car that can take you from one place to another in a faster and safer manner.

ROS (Robot Operating System) is the basis of the software framework. It provides services for device control and package management and also integrates the sensors and other hardware devices into a single environment.

The project has created a lot of opportunities for collaboration with industry which are being explored.
